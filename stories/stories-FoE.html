Fate of Existence

Her name is Laura, and she is the only survivor to survive this ongoing calamity. She stands at the center of various destroyed roads, between collapsed buildings, in the middle of a soon-to-be-destroyed planet.

Massive amounts of blood flood the streets, gradually evaporating away. The 4L Spyder Units continue roaming across Earth, crushing everything everywhere until all remains flattened to nothingness. Seeing everything filled with pure chaos and destruction, Laura can't handle it anymore. Seeing her friends gradually vanish and die in front of her eyes helplessly is the last thing she wants to experience in this war. She has lost so many loved ones in the past already. Tears begin sliding down her face.
