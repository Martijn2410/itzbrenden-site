Characters

Laura Lopez

"Zero Point Guardian of Time & Space"

Gender: Female

Pronouns: She/Her

Age: 14 - 500+

Sexual Orientation: Bisexual

Bio:

A young girl from Gaia, chosen to be the Zero Point’s Chosen One. After a difficult childhood, she vowed to protect the omni-verse from harm, determined that no one else should have to endure the suffering she once faced. With a direct connection to the Zero Point, she possesses an infinite well of energy that never runs dry. When paired with her Breaking Point ability, which amplifies her strength fiftyfold, defeating her becomes nearly impossible.

Demiko Minamoto

"Scarlet Dragon"

Gender: Female

Pronouns: She/Her

Age: 26 - 31

Sexual Orientation: Straight

Bio:

Eldest twin daughter of the Minamoto clan, she was raised and trained to become an elite warrior. Now a fugitive from the very people she once swore loyalty to, she seeks a better life beyond duty and bloodshed. Her original mission was to watch over and control the “Omni-being,” a task she carried out without question, until she could no longer endure witnessing the suffering inflicted upon the young girl. Defying her orders, she set the child free and chose to protect and take care of the girl herself.
