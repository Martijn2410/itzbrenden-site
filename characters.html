Laura Lopez

"Guardian of Time & Space"

Having endured her own share of hardships, Laura vowed to reshape reality, ensuring no one else would have to suffer as she did. Following the cataclysmic events of the Big Bang, Laura took it upon herself to safeguard the Omniverse. Now, she journeys across the Omniverse, seeking out worlds in need. Whether it's a personal conflict, a local threat, or a danger to the entire universe, Laura will always be there to lend her strength.Thanks to Laura's mastery of Zero Point energy, her power can only be described as limitless. With a direct connection to the Zero Point, she possesses an infinite well of energy that never runs dry. When paired with her Breaking Point ability, which amplifies her strength fiftyfold, defeating her becomes nearly impossible.
